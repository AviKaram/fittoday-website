"What 10,000 Students Taught Me About the Mental Health Crisis We're Not Ready For"

AK
Avi Karamchandani
Founder, FitToday · Duke University '29
Founder's Essay — Coming Soon

This essay is in progress.

In 2020, I was a high schooler watching students around me fall apart in ways that adults were not equipped to see or address. I built FitToday as my attempt to do something about that. What followed was five years of working with over 10,000 students across two countries, and learning things about the youth mental health crisis that I had not expected to learn.

This essay is where I put all of it down in one place. Subscribe below to get it when it goes live.
